Create Your Advertiser Account

Get started with Affitor in minutes. Creating an advertiser account is the first step to accessing our network of affiliate partners and growing your business through performance-based marketing.

What You'll Learn

  • How to register as an advertiser on Affitor
  • What information you'll need to provide
  • How to access your advertiser dashboard
  • Next steps after account creation

Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

  • A business or company email address
  • Basic information about your business
  • Products or services to promote
Note: You don't need a Stripe account yet — we'll set that up in a later step. Focus on creating your account first.

Registration Steps

Follow these simple steps to create your advertiser account:

1

Visit Affitor Management Portal

Navigate to the Affitor management portal:

affitor.com/affiliate-management/welcome-page
2

Select "Get started"

On the landing page, click the Get started button. This will direct you to the role selection page.

3

Select "I'm a Company"

Click the I'm a Company button. This will direct you to the advertiser registration flow.

Important: Make sure you select "I'm a Company" (Advertiser) and not "I'm a Partner" (Affiliate). Companies are businesses looking to work with affiliates; Partners are marketers looking to promote products.
4

Login with Company Email

Use your company or business email address to create your account. This ensures professional communication with partners, proper account verification, and access to business-level features.

Best practice: Use an email like partnerships@yourcompany.com rather than a personal email — it makes managing team access easier later.
5

Complete Initial Setup Wizard

As a first-time user, you'll be redirected to a setup wizard to configure your initial program. This one-time setup asks for:

setup-wizard.fields
# One-time, only for new accounts
program_name:  Acme Partners Program
website_url:   https://acme.com
logo:          company-logo.svg

What Happens Next?

After creating your account, you're ready to:

Create your program
Define your commission structure and program details.
Connect Stripe
Link your Stripe account for payment processing.
Add tracking
Install the tracking script on your website.
Get your first partner
Start working with affiliate marketers.
