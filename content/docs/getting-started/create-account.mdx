Get started with Affitor in under 2 minutes.

## Prerequisites

- A business or company email address
- Your company logo (PNG, JPG, or SVG)

## Quick steps

1. Visit [affitor.com/affiliate-management/welcome-page](https://affitor.com/affiliate-management/welcome-page)
2. Click **Get started**
3. Select **I'm a Company**
4. Log in with your company email
5. Complete the setup wizard (program name, website, logo)

You'll be taken directly to your advertiser dashboard.

For detailed instructions, see the [full account creation guide](/docs/advertisers/quickstart/create-account).
