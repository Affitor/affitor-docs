## Quick Overview

Affitor is an affiliate growth platform for SaaS companies. We help you:

- **Find partners** - Access a network of affiliates ready to promote your product
- **Track performance** - Accurate attribution from click to payment via Stripe
- **Pay automatically** - Commissions calculated and paid out via Stripe Connect

## Who is Affitor For?

### Advertisers (SaaS Companies)
If you're a SaaS company looking to grow through affiliate marketing, Affitor provides:
- Complete affiliate program management
- Automated tracking and attribution
- Partner network access
- Automated commission payouts

### Partners (Affiliates)
If you're an affiliate, influencer, or agency, Affitor offers:
- Access to quality SaaS programs
- Transparent tracking and reporting
- Automated commission payments
- Easy-to-use promotional tools
