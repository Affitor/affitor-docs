Affitor is an affiliate growth platform for SaaS companies.

We help you acquire customers through affiliates, influencers, and agencies. You only pay when they generate real revenue.

> **Key terms:**
> - **Advertiser** = The SaaS company running an affiliate program (also: brand, client)
> - **Partner** = The person or organization promoting your product (also: affiliate, influencer, agency)

## Not just tracking software

Most affiliate platforms give you tools to track referrals. That's it. You still need to find partners, negotiate deals, and manage relationships yourself.

Affitor is different. We help you:

- **Find partners.** Access a network of affiliates, influencers, and agencies ready to promote your product
- **Track performance.** Accurate attribution from click to payment via Stripe
- **Pay automatically.** Commissions calculated and paid out via Stripe Connect

One platform. From partner sourcing to payout.

## Performance-based pricing

No monthly subscription. No setup fees.

You pay 3.5% of the revenue your partners generate. If they don't generate revenue, you pay nothing.

**$10K guarantee:** Your first $10,000 in affiliate-driven revenue is fee-free. Zero risk to get started.

## Who uses Affitor?

Affitor is designed for SaaS teams that want to add a performance-based growth channel without increasing operational complexity.
Typical use cases include:

- SaaS founders launching their first affiliate program
- Growth teams looking to reduce CAC
- Companies expanding into US, UK, or EU markets

If you process payments through Stripe, you can use Affitor.
