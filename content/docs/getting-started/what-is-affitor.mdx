Affitor is an affiliate growth platform for SaaS companies.

It helps advertisers run partner programs with click, signup, and sale attribution — then manage commissions and payout operations through Affitor's workflow.

> **Key terms:**
> - **Advertiser** — the SaaS company running an affiliate program
> - **Partner** — the affiliate, creator, agency, or other promoter sending traffic

## How it fits into your stack

Affitor runs alongside your existing product stack.

```text
Partner shares link → visitor clicks → Affitor tracks click
                    ↓
              customer signs up → lead linked to partner
                    ↓
              customer pays → sale attributed via Sale API or Stripe metadata/webhooks
                    ↓
              commission + payout workflow handled in Affitor
```

You keep your own site and checkout. In the public Bill Flow model, you continue collecting payment in your own Stripe/payment setup.

## Performance-based pricing

- no monthly subscription
- no setup fee
- 3.5% platform fee on partner-generated revenue

Public pricing materials also describe a $10K fee-free threshold for affiliate-driven revenue.

## Who this is best for

Affitor is a strong fit for SaaS teams that want:
- a partner program without subscription-led affiliate software pricing
- clear tracking from click to revenue
- a Stripe Checkout or backend sale-tracking path they can implement cleanly
- commission and payout operations handled through one workflow
- a [CLI-first setup](/advertisers/cli/quickstart/) that gets tracking live in minutes (`npx affitor init`)
