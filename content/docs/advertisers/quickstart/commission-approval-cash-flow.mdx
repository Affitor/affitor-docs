## Payment Integration Methods

How you receive payments and how partners get paid depends on your checkout integration.

| Method | Stripe Account | How it works |
|--------|----------------|--------------|
| **Affitor Pay** | Affitor's Stripe | Affitor hosts checkout, collects payment, pays you after deducting commission + fee |
| **Bill Flow** | Your Stripe | You collect 100%, Affitor invoices you for commission + fee later |
| **Split Pay (Coming Soon)** | Your Stripe | Customer pays via your checkout, funds split automatically at payment (coming soon) |

---

## Affitor Pay Flow

*For advertisers without Stripe. Affitor handles everything.*

![Affitor Pay Flow](/images/affitor-pay-flow.png)
**Step by step:**

1. **Customer purchases** through Affitor-hosted checkout page
2. **Affitor collects** 100% of payment
3. **Transaction validated** (attribution, no duplicate, within window)
4. **You review** in dashboard (or auto-approve)
5. **Hold period** (7-21 days, configurable)
6. **If no refund:** Affitor deducts commission + platform fee, pays you the rest
7. **Partner commission** moves to Withdrawable balance

**Best for:** Fast setup, no Stripe account needed.

---

## Bill Flow

*You collect everything, Affitor invoices later.*

**Step by step:**

1. **Customer purchases** through your existing checkout
2. **You receive 100%** of payment via your Stripe
3. **Affitor tracks** and validates the transaction
4. **You review** in dashboard and approve
5. **Affitor sends weekly invoice** for: Partner commission + Platform fee (3.5%)
6. **You pay invoice**
7. **Hold period** (7-21 days)
8. **If no refund:** Commission moves to Partner's Withdrawable balance
9. **Partner withdraws** or keeps balance

**Best for:** Keep existing checkout flow, pay commission later.

## Transaction Validation

Every transaction is validated before commission is calculated:

| Check | What Affitor verifies |
|-------|----------------------|
| **Valid attribution** | Affiliate link was used |
| **No duplicate** | Customer not already attributed to another partner |
| **Within attribution window** | Purchase within 60 days of click |
| **Matching amount** | Payment matches expected value |

If validation fails, the transaction is flagged for your review.

---

## Approval Process

You control which transactions become payable commissions.

### Auto-Approve

Transactions approved automatically after validation passes.

- Faster processing
- Less manual work
- Best for: High-volume, low-risk products

### Manual Review

You review each transaction before approval.

- Full control
- Catch issues early
- Best for: High-ticket items, new programs

**From your dashboard:**

1. Go to Transactions
2. Review pending items
3. Click **Approve** or **Reject**

### Rejection Reasons

- Customer requested refund
- Fraudulent activity detected
- Partner policy violation
- Invalid or test transaction

Rejected transactions don't generate commission.

## Hold Period

Commissions are held before becoming withdrawable to protect against refunds.

| Hold Period | Use case |
|-------------|----------|
| **7 days** | Low-risk products, established partners |
| **14 days** | Standard SaaS subscriptions |
| **21 days** | High-ticket items, refund-prone products |

**During hold period:**
- Commission shows as "Pending" in partner dashboard
- Customer refund → Commission cancelled automatically
- No refund → Commission moves to "Withdrawable"

---

## Partner Payouts

Once commission clears hold period:

1. **Commission becomes Withdrawable** in partner's Affitor balance
2. **Partner chooses:**
   - Withdraw to bank/payment method
   - Keep balance in Affitor

**Payout frequency:** Bi-weekly (every 2 weeks)

Affitor handles all partner payouts. You don't manage individual transfers.

## Refund Handling

| When refund happens | What happens to commission |
|---------------------|---------------------------|
| During hold period | Commission cancelled automatically |
| After hold period, before payout | Commission reversed from partner balance |
| After payout | Deducted from partner's next payout |
