Creating an advertiser account is the first step to accessing our partner network and growing your business through performance-based marketing.

## Prerequisites

- A business or company email address
- Basic information about your business
- Products or services to promote

> [!NOTE]
> You don't need a Stripe account yet. We'll set that up in a later step.

## Registration

### Step 1: Visit the management portal

Go to **[affitor.com/affiliate-management/welcome-page](https://affitor.com/affiliate-management/welcome-page)** and click **Get started**.

### Step 2: Select "I'm a Company"

This directs you to the advertiser registration flow.

> [!WARNING]
> Make sure you select "I'm a Company" (Advertiser), not "I'm a Partner" (Affiliate). Companies run affiliate programs. Partners promote products.

### Step 3: Log in with your company email

Use your business email address (e.g., `partnerships@yourcompany.com`). This ensures professional communication with partners and easier team management later.

### Step 4: Complete the setup wizard

After login, you'll go through a one-time setup wizard:

- **Program Name**: Your affiliate program name (e.g., "Acme Partners Program")
- **Website URL**: Your business website
- **Logo**: Your company or program logo

After completing the wizard, you'll have full access to your advertiser dashboard.

## After account creation

1. **Create your affiliate program**: Define commission structure and program details
2. **Connect Stripe**: Link your Stripe account for payment processing
3. **Add tracking**: Install the tracking script on your website
4. **Invite partners**: Start working with affiliate marketers

## FAQ

<details>
<summary>Can I use a personal email address?</summary>

Yes, but we recommend a business email for professionalism and easier team management. You can update your email later in account settings.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Is there a cost to create an account?</summary>

No. Account creation is free. You only pay platform fees and partner commissions when you make sales through the platform.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Do I need to provide payment information now?</summary>

No. You'll connect your Stripe account later when setting up your program.

</details>
