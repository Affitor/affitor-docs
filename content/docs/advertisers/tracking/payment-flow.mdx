## Step-by-Step Flow

### 1. Partner Shares Link

Partner promotes your product with their unique link:

```
https://yoursite.com?aff=PARTNER123
```

### 2. Customer Clicks

Customer clicks the link and lands on your site.

**What happens:**
- Affitor tracker detects `?aff=PARTNER123`
- Generates unique `click_id` and stores in cookie
- Creates customer-partner relationship in database
- Click event recorded

### 3. Customer Browses

Customer explores your site. Cookies persist for 60 days.

**Attribution window:** 60 days from first click.     

### 4. Customer Signs Up

Customer creates an account.

**Your code calls:**
```javascript
if (window.affitor) {
  await window.affitor.signup('user_123', 'customer@example.com');
} else {
  window.affitorQueue = window.affitorQueue || [];
  window.affitorQueue.push(['signup', 'user_123', 'customer@example.com']);
}
```

**What happens:**
- Lead event linked to partner via cookies
- Your signup identifier is associated with the tracked customer record (`customerKey` in `signup()`, `customer_key` in the API)
- Partner sees: 1 click → 1 signup

### 5. Customer Purchases

Customer completes payment through your checkout.

If you use Stripe Checkout, include metadata like this:

```javascript
metadata: {
  program_id: 'YOUR_PROGRAM_ID',
  affitor_click_id: 'cust_xyz',
  affitor_customer_key: currentUser.id  // Your internal user ID (same as customerKey in signup)
}
```

### 6. Sale Is Recorded

Affitor receives the completed sale either from your Stripe webhook flow or from `POST /api/v1/track/sale`.

**What happens:**
- Affitor receives payment confirmation
- Reads `program_id`
- Reads `affitor_click_id` and/or `customer_key` / `affitor_customer_key`
- Looks up the tracked customer record
- Verifies attribution is within the active window
- Creates commission record

### 7. Commission Calculated

Based on your program settings:

| Setting | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Commission rate | 30% |
| Sale amount | $100 (10000 cents) |
| Commission | $30 (3000 cents) |

:::tip[API uses cents]
When using the [Server-Side Sale API](/docs/advertisers/tracking/quickstart-integration/#step-3--track-sales), amounts must be in **cents** as a positive integer. For example, `$99.99` = `amount_cents: 9999`. Stripe metadata does not require this — Stripe handles amounts natively.
:::

### 8. Transaction Validated

Affitor checks:

| Check | Validation |
|-------|------------|
| No duplicate | Customer not already attributed |
| Matching program | Program ID is valid |

### 9. Hold Period

Commission enters hold period (7-21 days, configurable).

**During hold:**
- Commission shows as "Pending"
- If customer refunds → commission cancelled
- If no refund → commission clears

### 10. Partner Payout

After hold period clears:

- Commission moves to "Withdrawable"
- Bi-weekly payout cycle processes
- Partner receives funds via Bank/Wise/Stripe

## Flow by Payment Method

### Bill Flow

```
Customer → Your checkout → You collect payment
                                ↓
                          Affitor tracks conversion
                          Weekly invoice: commission + platform fee
                                ↓
                          You pay invoice
                                ↓
                          Hold period clears
                                ↓
                          Partner receives payout
```

---

## Subscription Flow

For recurring payments, the flow repeats each billing cycle:

### Month 1 (Initial Purchase)

```
Click → Signup → Subscribe $100/mo
                      ↓
              Commission: $30
              Hold: 14 days
              Partner paid: $30
```

### Month 2+ (Renewal)

```
Subscription renews $100
         ↓
   Commission: $30 (if within duration)
   Hold: 14 days
   Partner paid: $30
```

### Commission Duration

| Duration Setting | Partner Earns On |
|-----------------|------------------|
| One-time | First payment only |
| 3 months | Months 1-3 |
| 12 months | Months 1-12 |
| Lifetime | All future payments |

**Example (12-month duration):**
- Month 1: ✅ Commission paid
- Month 6: ✅ Commission paid
- Month 12: ✅ Commission paid
- Month 13: ❌ No commission (outside duration)

## Refund Handling

| When Refund Happens | Commission Status |
|---------------------|-------------------|
| During hold period | Cancelled automatically |
| After hold, before payout | Reversed from balance |
| After payout | Deducted from next payout |
