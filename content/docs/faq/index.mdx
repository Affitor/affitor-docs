## Pricing & Fees


### How much does Affitor cost?

Affitor charges a **3.5% platform fee** on affiliate-generated revenue. That's it.

- No setup fees
- No monthly subscriptions
- No retainers
- No hidden charges


### What about the $10K revenue waiver?
    Affitor's pricing page currently describes a **$10K fee-free threshold** for affiliate-driven revenue. If you need confirmation on your program's commercial terms, contact the Affitor team before launch.

### How are fees calculated?

Fees are calculated on **actual received revenue** after refunds, chargebacks, and failed transactions. Taxes and payment processing fees (like Stripe's 2.9%) are borne by you as the advertiser, not deducted from affiliate or platform economics.

**Example: $30/month SaaS subscription**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Gross revenue | $30.00 |
| Stripe fee (2.9% + $0.30) | -$1.17 |
| Affiliate commission (30%) | -$9.00 |
| Affitor platform fee (3.5%) | -$1.05 |
| **You keep** | **$18.78** |

**Your effective CAC:** $10.05 (affiliate + Affitor)
**Your net margin:** 62.6% of gross revenue


### When do I pay?
    In the current public Bill Flow model, advertiser billing is handled through Affitor's invoice process after tracked conversions are validated.

## How It Works


### Do I need to change my payment infrastructure?
    No. Affitor is **not** the merchant of record. We integrate with your existing payment system (Stripe, LemonSqueezy, Paddle, etc.). You continue processing customer payments exactly as you do today.

### How does tracking work?
    Affitor uses modern, first-party tracking methods designed for privacy-conscious browsers. Unlike legacy platforms that rely on third-party cookie redirects (which Safari, Firefox, and ad blockers often block), our tracking is reliable across all devices and browsers.

### How fast can I launch?

Integration speed depends on your stack and checkout setup. Many teams can start quickly with the script-based tracker or existing Stripe Checkout metadata flow, while custom backend integrations may take longer.


### What commission structures are supported?

- **One-time commissions** (e.g., flat $50 per sale)

## Payouts & Commissions


### Who pays the affiliates?

Affitor handles partner payouts on your behalf:

1. **Sale happens** — Customer purchases via affiliate link
2. **Invoice sent** — You pay commission + platform fee
3. **Funds received** — Money moves to Affitor's holding balance
4. **Hold period clears** — 7-21 days (configurable)
5. **Bi-weekly payout** — Affitor pays partner

This removes operational burden from your team while ensuring partners get paid reliably.


### What payout methods are available for partners?

Partners can receive payouts via:

- **Bank transfer** (global)
- **Wise** (1-2 business days)
- **Stripe** (where available)


### How often are partners paid?
    **Bi-weekly** (every 2 weeks). Partners receive all cleared commissions in their Withdrawable balance on a fixed cycle. Minimum payout threshold: **$5**.

### What happens if there's a refund?

**Bill Flow:** Refunds are reconciled through Affitor's commission and payout process. If a commission has not yet cleared, it is cancelled or reversed. If it has already been paid out, the adjustment is applied against the partner's balance or future payout.

Affitor's 3.5% platform fee and partner commission are reconciled together when the refund is processed.

## Troubleshooting / Integration


### "Test Click Tracking" shows "No Events Detected"

This means the dashboard hasn't received a pageview event from your site yet. Run through this checklist:

1. **Verify the script is loading** — Open DevTools → Network tab, filter by `affitor-tracker`. You should see a successful (200) request.
2. **Check your Program ID** — The `data-affitor-program-id` must match the ID shown in your dashboard Settings.
3. **Visit with a `?aff=` parameter** — Pageview tracking activates when a visitor arrives via an affiliate link.
4. **Disable ad blockers** — Some extensions can block tracking scripts. Test in an incognito window.
5. **HTTPS is required** — Affitor cookies require a secure context.
6. **Check the browser console** — Enable debug mode and look for `[Affitor]` messages.

See the full [Pageview Tracker guide](/docs/advertisers/tracking/pageview-tracker-click/) for setup details.


### Console shows no Affitor debug messages

- **Debug mode not enabled** — Add `data-affitor-debug="true"` to the script tag
- **Script not loaded** — Check DevTools → Network for the tracker script request
- **Content Security Policy (CSP) blocking** — Ensure they allow scripts from `api.affitor.com`
- **Ad blocker interference** — Test in a clean browser profile


### Cookies not being set (affitor_click_id missing)

- **No `?aff=` parameter** — The cookie is only set when a visitor arrives via `?aff=PARTNER_CODE`
- **Not on HTTPS** — Cookies require a secure context in production
- **Browser privacy settings** — Some browsers may block first-party cookies
- **CSP blocking** — Your Content Security Policy headers might be blocking cookie creation


### Integration works in development but not in production

- **Wrong tracker script URL** — Production must use `affitor-tracker.js`, not `affitor-tracker-uat.js`
- **CSP headers** — Production often has stricter policies. Ensure `api.affitor.com` is allowed
- **HTTPS required** — Production must use HTTPS for cookies to work
- **Different domain** — Cookies are domain-scoped. Ensure your production domain matches your Affitor config

If issues persist, contact **support@affitor.com** with your Program ID, browser console screenshots, and steps to reproduce.
