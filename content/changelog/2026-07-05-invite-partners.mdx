![Invite partners with a pre-written, terms-based email](/changelog/invite-partners/invite-compose.png)

You can now invite partners into your program directly — type emails, import a CSV, or pick people already performing in your other Affitor programs. When you get to the email, it's **already written from your program's real terms**: commission model, attribution window, payout threshold. Edit it freely (we save your version per program), check the live preview, and send. Invited partners skip the application queue — they're active the moment they accept, tracking link in hand.

A few things shipped alongside:

- **One partners table.** The All view now mixes active partners, applications, invites, and rejections in a single list with status badges — applications float to the top, and you can approve or decline right from the row.
- **Resend and cancel** any pending invite from the same table.
- **Invite suggestions** from your other programs, with per-program filters and search.
- Invites expire after 30 days, and failed email deliveries are flagged with the reason.
