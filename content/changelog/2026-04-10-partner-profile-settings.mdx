---
title: Partner profile and security settings
date: '2026-04-10'
category: Dashboard
benefit: Partners can now update their profile, avatar, and password without contacting support.
---

Partners get a proper Settings page. Two tabs: **Account** for name, email, payout details, and avatar; **Security** for password and session management.

Avatar uploads go straight to S3 and show up across the dashboard immediately. Password changes are validated against the same rules as signup, so there's no surprise rejection.

If you've been emailing support to update partner details, that's no longer needed.
