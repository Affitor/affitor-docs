Get started with Affitor in under 2 minutes.

## Prerequisites

- A Google account
- Your company logo (PNG, JPG, or SVG - max 1800×1800px)

## Step 1: Go to Affitor

Visit [affitor.com/get-started](https://affitor.com/get-started)

## Step 2: Sign in with Google

Click **Continue with Google** and select your account.

{/* SCREENSHOT: @trinhnguyen8010 - Google sign-in screen */}

## Step 3: Choose your role

Select **Advertiser** to create and manage an affiliate program.

{/* SCREENSHOT: @trinhnguyen8010 - Role selection screen */}

## Step 4: Enter program details

Fill in your basic information:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Company Name | Your company or product name |
| Website URL | Your product's landing page |
| Tagline | Short description (optional) |
| Logo | Your company logo |
| Terms of Service URL | Link to your affiliate terms (optional) |
| Resources URL | Link to marketing materials (optional) |

{/* SCREENSHOT: @trinhnguyen8010 - Program details form */}

## Step 5: Start setup

After submitting, you’ll be taken directly to your home dashboard, where you can complete your program configuration and launch your first affiliate program with Affitor.

## What's next

You've created your account. Now let's set up your affiliate program:
