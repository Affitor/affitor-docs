## When you pay

The 3.5% platform fee is collected when you pay out commissions to partners. No separate invoice, no delayed billing — it happens automatically alongside partner payouts.

## Cash Flow Timing

Commissions have a 7-21 day hold period before partner payout. This protects you against refunds and chargebacks.

[→ See full cash flow details](/advertisers/quickstart/commission-approval-cash-flow)

Your first $10,000 in affiliate-driven revenue is fee-free.

Launch your program, test the platform, and validate results before paying anything to Affitor.
