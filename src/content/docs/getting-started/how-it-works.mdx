![How Affitor Works - 7 Step Affiliate Lifecycle](/images/how-it-works-diagram.png)

## The affiliate lifecycle

<Steps>
<Step title="Advertiser creates program">
    You set up your affiliate program in minutes. Define commission rates, approval rules, and payout terms.
</Step>
<Step title="Partner joins & gets link">
    Partners apply to your program. Once approved, each partner receives a unique tracking link.
</Step>
<Step title="Partner promotes">
    Partners share their links via content, social media, newsletters, or direct outreach.
</Step>
<Step title="Customer clicks & visits">
    A potential customer clicks the affiliate link and lands on your website. Affitor records the referral.
</Step>
<Step title="Customer converts & pays">
    The customer signs up and completes a payment. Affitor captures the conversion through your sale tracking setup — either Stripe metadata/webhooks or the server-side sale API.
</Step>
<Step title="Commission calculated">
    Affitor calculates the commission based on your program rules. The commission enters a 7-21 day hold period.
</Step>
<Step title="Partner gets paid">
    After the hold period, cleared commissions become withdrawable and are paid out to partners through Affitor's payout process.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Key points

**Flexible integration paths** — You can track sales through Stripe metadata/webhooks or the server-side sale API, depending on your checkout setup.

**60-day attribution window** — Partners get credit for conversions within 60 days of the initial click.

**Real-time dashboard** — Both you and your partners see performance data as it happens.
