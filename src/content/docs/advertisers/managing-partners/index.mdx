# Managing Partners

Effectively managing your affiliate partners is key to running a successful program. Learn how to review applications, maintain quality standards, and build strong partner relationships.

## Partner Management Guides

- **[Approve & Reject Partners](/advertisers/managing-partners/approve-reject/)** - Review and manage partner applications

## Partner Lifecycle

### 1. Application
Partners apply to join your affiliate program through the Affitor platform.

### 2. Review
You review applications based on your criteria and quality standards.

### 3. Approval/Rejection
Approve qualified partners or reject applications that don't meet your standards.

### 4. Onboarding
Approved partners receive access to promotional materials and tracking links.

### 5. Ongoing Management
Monitor partner performance and maintain program quality.

## Best Practices

- Set clear program requirements and communicate them upfront
- Review applications promptly (within 24-48 hours)
- Provide clear rejection reasons to help partners improve
- Monitor partner performance regularly
- Maintain open communication with your partners

## Quality Control

Maintaining program quality helps ensure:

- Brand alignment with your values
- High-quality traffic and conversions
- Positive customer experience
- Sustainable program growth
