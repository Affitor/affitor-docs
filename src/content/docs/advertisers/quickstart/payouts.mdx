# Payouts

Affitor handles all partner payouts. This guide explains payout schedules, methods, and how the process works.

---

## How Payouts Work

Sale happens → Invoice sent to advertiser → Funds to Affitor balance → Hold period → Partner payout


1. **Transaction completed** – Customer purchases via affiliate link
2. **Invoice sent** – Advertiser pays commission + platform fee
   - Bill Flow: Weekly summary invoice
3. **Funds received** – Money moves to Affitor's holding balance
4. **Hold period clears** – Commission becomes withdrawable (7-21 days)
5. **Bi-weekly payout** – Partner receives payment

You don't need to manage individual partner payments. Affitor handles everything.

## Payout Schedule

| Frequency | Schedule |
|-----------|----------|
| **Bi-weekly** | Every 2 weeks |

Partners receive payouts on a fixed bi-weekly cycle for all cleared commissions in their Withdrawable balance.

---

## Payout Methods

Partners choose their preferred payout method in their Affitor dashboard:

| Method | Availability | Processing time |
|--------|--------------|-----------------|
| Bank transfer | Global | 2-5 business days |
| Wise | Global | 1-2 business days |
| Stripe | Where available | 1-2 business days |

Partners manage their own payout settings. You don't need to collect banking details.

---

## Payout Threshold

Minimum payout: **$5**

Partners working through Affitor often promote multiple programs simultaneously. Affitor aggregates earnings across all programs into a single balance, making payouts efficient for everyone.

---

## Partner Balance States

Commission moves through these states:

| State | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Pending** | Within hold period, subject to refund |
| **Withdrawable** | Cleared hold period, ready for payout |
| **Paid** | Sent to partner's bank/Wise/Stripe |

Partners see all states in their dashboard with full transaction history.

---

## What Partners See

In their Affitor dashboard, partners can view:

- **Pending balance** – Commissions still in hold period
- **Withdrawable balance** – Ready to be paid out
- **Payout history** – All past payments with dates and amounts
- **Transaction details** – Individual sales and commission earned

---

## Refunds After Payout

If a customer refunds after commission was paid to partner:

| Scenario | What happens |
|----------|--------------|
| Partner has balance | Refund amount deducted from next payout |
| Partner has no balance | Negative balance carried forward |

This protects you from paying commission on refunded sales.

---

## Your Role

As an advertiser, you:

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Approve/reject transactions | Manage individual partner payments |
| Set hold period length | Collect partner banking details |
| Monitor commission costs | Process payout transfers |

Affitor handles the operational complexity of paying partners worldwide.

---

## Payout Fees

| Fee | Who pays |
|-----|----------|
| Payout processing | Affitor (included in platform fee) |
| Currency conversion | Partner (if applicable) |
| Bank receiving fees | Partner's bank (varies) |

No additional fees charged to advertisers for partner payouts.
