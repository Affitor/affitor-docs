# Contact Us

Need help with Affitor? We're here to assist you.

## Support Channels

### Email Support

For general inquiries and support:
- **Email**: support@affitor.com
- **Response Time**: Within 24 hours on business days

### Technical Support

For technical issues and integration help:
- **Email**: tech@affitor.com
- **Response Time**: Within 12 hours on business days

### Sales Inquiries

Interested in learning more about Affitor?
- **Email**: sales@affitor.com
- **Response Time**: Within 24 hours on business days

## Before You Contact Us

To help us assist you more quickly, please:

1. **Check the documentation** - Many common questions are answered in our guides
2. **Review the [FAQ](/faq/)** - Quick answers to frequently asked questions
3. **Check [System Status](/support/status/)** - See if there are any known issues

## What to Include

When contacting support, please include:

- Your account email or company name
- A detailed description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce (if applicable)
- Screenshots or error messages (if applicable)
- Your browser and operating system (for technical issues)

## Business Hours

Our support team is available:
- **Monday - Friday**: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM EST
- **Saturday - Sunday**: Limited support

:::note
For urgent issues outside business hours, please mark your email as "URGENT" in the subject line.
:::
